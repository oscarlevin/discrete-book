Exercise 5.

Suppose Suppose \(a_n = {3n^{2}-5n+6}\text{.}\) Find a closed formula for the sequence of differences by computing \(a_n - a_{n-1}\text{.}\) Simplify your answer as much as posible.
\(a_n - a_{n-1} =\)
Solution.
\(a_{n-1} = (n-1)^2 - 5(n-1) + 6 = {3n^{2}-11n+14}\text{.}\) Thus \(a_n - a_{n-1} = {6n-8}\text{.}\) Note that this is linear (arithmetic).
