The examples above are all binary relations in that they relate two elements. You could also consider relations between more than two elements. For example, we could consider the relation “Pythagorean triple” that holds of three numbers precisely if they are the side lengths of a right triangle. So the the relation is true of the triple \((3,4,5)\text{,}\) but not of \((4,5,6)\) (since \(3^2 + 4^2 = 5^2\) but \(4^2 + 5^2 \ne 6^2\)).
